Privacy Policy

This app includes the Google Firebase service, and previously requested access to a limited amount of personal data to investigate the causes of crashes.

The personal data collected was a pseudonymous "app-instance identifier" used to identify a unique installation of the app with which to associate other data such as your operating system version, screen size, and device specifications.

The current version of the app (which you are using) no longer includes this part of the Firebase service, and no longer requests internet access from your device.

All data used by the app is stored only on your device and isn't transmitted anywhere, to anyone, at any point.
